HR Analytic Dashboard

An **interactive HR dashboard** providing insights into employee attrition, demographics, job satisfaction, and key HR metrics.  
Helps organizations **identify trends, pinpoint risks, and make data-driven decisions** for talent retention.

Key Metrics
- **Total Employees:** 961  
- **Attrition Count:** 133 (13.84%)  
- **Active Employees:** 828  
- **Average Age:** 37 years  

 Visual Highlights
- Department-wise Attrition (Pie Chart)  
- Employees by Age Group (Histogram)  
- Attrition by Gender (Bar Chart)  
- Job Satisfaction by Role (Table/Heatmap)  
- Education-wise Attrition (Horizontal Bar Chart)  
- Attrition Rate by Gender & Age (Donut Charts)  

Purpose
- Identify high-attrition departments and roles  
- Understand demographic factors influencing turnover  
- Assess job satisfaction levels  
- Support strategic retention decisions
